Grave of the Fireflies (1988)

Description

1988 | 1hr 29min | Animated

Grave of the Fireflies (Japanese: 火垂るの墓) is a Japanese animated war tragedy film based on a 1967 short story by Akiyuki Nosaka. It tells the story of two siblings and war orphans, Seita and Setsuko, and their desperate struggle to survive during the final months of the Second World War.
